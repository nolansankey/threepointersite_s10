Welcome to Three Pointer Season 10!

We've come a long way since 2017.


How to play: Each day I will post 3 questions about that day's sporting events (ex. Will the Golden State Warriors score 112 or more points against the L.A. Lakers? Will the Colorado Avalanche and New York Rangers combine for 6 or more goals? Will the New York Jets beat the Green Bay Packers?). You earn one point for each question you get correct and if you nail all three you make a Three Pointer and earn an extra three points for a total of six. For season 10, the 10 player(s) with the most points at the end of the week get paid out and there are also season long prizes.
